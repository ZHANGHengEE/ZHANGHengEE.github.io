Heng ZHANG - Biography

Heng Zhang (Graduate Student Member, IEEE) received the B.Eng. degree in 2017, and the M.Eng. degree in 2020, both in Electrical Engineering and Automation, from Taiyuan University of Technology, Taiyuan, Shanxi, China. From 2021 to 2022, he was a research assistant with the Chinese University of Hong Kong, Hong Kong. He is currently working toward the Ph.D. degree in electrical and electronic engineering from the University of Hong Kong, Hong Kong.

He was awarded the Research Postgraduate Student Innovation Award 2024/25 by the Graduate School of the University of Hong Kong in recognition of his research contributions. Additionally, he received the Student Design Competition Award at the IEEE WPTCE 2025.

His research interests include sensing technology, medical robotic design and control, wireless power transfer, and power electronics.
